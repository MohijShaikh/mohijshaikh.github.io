FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need prior experience to start?
+
No. All three courses start from the basics. The Excel course assumes you know how to open Excel. The MySQL course assumes zero SQL knowledge. Power BI assumes no BI experience. If you already have some background, you can skip ahead.
Are these courses based on real data?
+
Yes — all datasets are anonymised versions of real e-commerce and quick-commerce systems I've built. You'll work on inventory, P&L, SLA tracking, and order analytics — the same kind of data that goes to CFOs and Founders.
Why do videos require login?
+
The first lesson of each course is free and publicly viewable. Full course videos are gated behind login to protect course content and ensure only enrolled students access paid material. Creating an account is free — enrollment gives you full access.
Do I get lifetime access?
+
Yes. Once purchased, the course is yours forever — including future updates. I update content whenever Power BI, MySQL, or Excel have significant changes relevant to the course material.
What jobs can these courses prepare me for?
+
Data Analyst, MIS Executive, BI Analyst, BI Developer, Business Intelligence Manager. The bundle covers the full stack you need — from writing SQL queries to delivering executive dashboards to senior management.
What order should I take the courses?
+
Recommended order: Excel → MySQL → Power BI. This builds your skills logically — spreadsheet thinking first, then database querying, then bringing it all together in Power BI. If you buy the bundle, you'll have access to all three from day one.
